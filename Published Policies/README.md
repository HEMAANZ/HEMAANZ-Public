# Published Policy Documents

Published policies are promoted from draft versions after at least one round of consultation has been completed, as per the consultation policy. 
Published policies are documents that describe HEMA New Zealand's established position regarding topics mentioned therein;
To achieve Affiliate status, local clubs must consider all relevant policies and incorporate them into their own policy documents - these are minimum standards and local clubs may exert further control (insofar as they don't directly contradict any policies published by HEMAANZ).
